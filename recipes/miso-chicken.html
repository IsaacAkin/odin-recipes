Miso Chicken Recipe


Description

Made with just a few simple ingredients, this air fryer miso chicken thighs dish comes together in under 30 minutes. And if you always forget to marinate your chicken, this recipe doesn't require any marinating! It only takes a few seconds to whip up the garlic miso sauce, and then you can rub it all over the chicken thighs before cooking.


Ingredients

  • 8 Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 tbsp sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • 4 tbsp minced green onions (4 green stalks)
  • 3 tbsp white miso paste
  • 3 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic

Steps

  1. Combine marinade ingredients. In a medium-sized bowl, add the sake, mirin, miso, honey, soy sauce, and garlic. Mix and add the chicken.
  2. Massage the chicken well with the miso sauce.
  3. Line the air fryer tray or basket with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  4. Place the chicken in a single layer. Put all the miso glaze on the tray. This will ensure a juicy chicken.
  5. Air fry at 320 degrees for 20 minutes, flipping halfway. Broil the chicken for 2 minutes to get a nice crust/color. The internal temperature of the meat should be 165 degrees.
  6. Baste it with leftover pan juices, serve, and enjoy!
